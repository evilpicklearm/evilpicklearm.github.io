Atopic Dermatitis
Symptoms
Red rash to grayish brown especially at the wrist, the ankle and the elbow. If chronic, the skin will be dry, thick and dark circles will be visible.
Initial Treatment
- Avoid wearing clothes with rough textures that can causes irritation, ex. Synthetic Fabric or Wool.(Suitable clothes are cotton clothes)
- Avoid staying under direct sunlight.
- Avoid stimulants that can cause irritation and scratching.
Consultation-needed cases
- Feeling ill
- Having an effect on sleeping or daily routines
- Being infected or/with visible abscess


Other skin lesions
Doctor consultation within 24 hours is appropiate. The affecting skin lesions could be life threatening.
